Work Ethic:

Work ethic is important to have in everyday life, it is good to use in school and at work. Work ethic is important to have especially if you want to get a good job. Work ethics are characteristics of hard work that many jobs look for when trying to hire someone new. If you have these important characteristics you will get better grades and a good job. No matter how important work ethic is many people do not have it. Do not be lazy with your work and give 100 percent in everything.

Here are some attributes:

  • Being Dependable
  • Good Attitude
  • Being Determined
  • Responsiblity
  • Productiveness
  • Good Attendance
  • Cooperation
  • Communciation
