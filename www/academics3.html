Work Ethic:

Work ethic is important to have in everyday life, it is good to use in school, at work, and even at home when doing chores. Work ethic is important to have especially if you want to get a good job. Work ethics are characteristics of hard work that many jobs look for when trying to hire someone new. If you have these important characteristics you will get better grades, a good job, and even more just from being a hard worker. No matter how important work ethic is many people do not have it. Work ethics are also skills to have even if you do not have a job or go to school. Do not be lazy with your work and give 100 percent.

Here are some attributes:

  • Be Respectiful
  • Being Dependable
  • Good Attitude
  • Being Determined
  • Responsiblity
  • Productiveness
  • Good Attendance
  • Cooperation
  • Communciation
