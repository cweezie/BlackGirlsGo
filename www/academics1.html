Time Mangement:

Time management is a very important aspect of everyone's academic life. Here are some suggestions on how to use your time wisely. When assigned a project you should first look at the due date and then plan what part of the project you are going to do each day. So make a schedule or an outline of what you are going to work on and when you are going to work on that. When studying for a test or quiz you should not cram because you cannot retain the information that way. Depending on the material and how well you comprehend the information you should start studying about 5-7 days before the test. The amount of studying you do each day depends on how far ahead you study. If Sierra starts studying a week in advance she might only study for 40 minutes each day. However, if Tiana starts studying 3 days in advance she might study for about an hour and a half each day.
