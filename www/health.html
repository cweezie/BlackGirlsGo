Time management is a very important aspect of everyone's academic life. Here are some suggestions on how to use your time wisely. When assigned a project you should first look at the due date and then plan what part of the project you are going to do each day up to the due date. So make a schedule or an outline of what you are going to work on and when you are going to work on that. When studying for a test or quiz you should not cram because you cannot retain the information that way. Depending on the material and how well you comprehend the information you should start studying about 5-7 days before the test. The amount of studying you do each day depends on how far ahead you study. If Sierra starts studying a week in advances he might only study for 40 minutes each day. However, if Tiana starts studying 3 days in advance she might study for about an hour and a half each day.

As a teenager, we are always busy, so make sure to have a calendar or planner to organize yourself. It could be a Google Calendar, plan book, planning app, or any other method that works for you. You should always have a database to refer to. Make sure to keep all of your school work in the exclusive binder or folder for that class. Use dividers to separate your homework from classwork and classwork from completed tests/ quizzes. Don't forget to carry pencil cases loaded with pencils, pens, colored pencils, sharpener, white out, and any other materials you feel are beneficial to your learning. In your locker, use a locker shelf to keep your things easily accessible. When moving between classes, if you need to stop and quickly pick something up, you won't have to dig for it and as a cause be late to your next class. Use magnets to hang notes or reminders about tests, quizzes, dues dates, or after school activities.

Work ethic is important to have in everyday life, it is good to use in school, at work, and even at home when doing chores. Work ethic is important to have especially if you want to get a good job. Work ethics are characteristics of hard work that many jobs look for when trying to hire someone new. If you have these important characteristics you will get better grades, a good job, and even more just from being a hard worker. No matter how important work ethic is many people do not have it. Work ethics are also skills to have even if you do not have a job or go to school. Do not be lazy with your work and give 100 percent.
